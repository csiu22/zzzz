ASMR

Autonomous sensory meridian response (ASMR) is a euphoric experience characterized by a static-like or tingling sensation on the skin that typically begins on the scalp and moves down the back of the neck and upper spine, precipitating relaxation. - Wikipedia

Binaural Beats

A binaural beat is an auditory illusion perceived when each ear hears a different pure-tone sine wave. In theory, brainwaves can synchronize with the periodicity of these external stimuli and depending on the frequency of the beats, alter mood, concentration, and relaxation.
